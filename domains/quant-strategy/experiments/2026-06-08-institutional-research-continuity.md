## Workflow Split

Daily tasks:

- Capture new public information and market signals.
- Use institutional overlays in daily strategy, execution, and post-close audit.
- Keep outputs tied to market fear, trend confirmation, concentration, and stop rules.

Weekly task:

- Read the four institutional sources more deeply.
- Build a source index of new articles and research themes.
- Extract reusable frameworks.
- Map ideas to `trend_aligned_entry`, `flow_fragility`, `AI_quality/capex_cycle`, `factor_macro_exposure`, `bottleneck_watch`, and replay/backtest plans.
- Update references, hypotheses, daily notes, or experiments as appropriate.

## Promotion Rules

- One-off research insight: `memory/daily/`.
- Reusable framework: `references/`.
- Unproven strategy idea: `memory/hypotheses.md`.
- Backtest or replay candidate: `experiments/`.
- Stable rule: `memory/decisions.md` only after validation.

## Guardrails

- Do not treat institutional commentary as a direct trade signal.
- Do not bypass the market fear gate, trend confirmation, portfolio concentration, or stop rules.
- Separate facts, source opinions, and strategy inferences.
- Keep account credentials, brokerage details, and private information out of memory.
