1. Start from AI model scaling and inference demand.
2. Translate demand into data-center bottlenecks: compute, interconnect, optical modules, memory/storage, power, and cloud deployment.
3. Identify which bottleneck is becoming scarce next.
4. Map the bottleneck into public equities and supply-chain layers.

6. Look for market dislocations after earnings drops, sector pullbacks, or narrative rotation.
7. Maintain caution when a hot theme becomes crowded or when price runs ahead of verified fundamentals.
